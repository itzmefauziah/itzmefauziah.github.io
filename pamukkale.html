Pamukkale

Pamukkale, meaning "Cotton Castle," is a natural wonder of terraces formed by mineral-rich thermal waters. It’s also home to the ancient city of Hierapolis.

Main Attractions

  • Thermal Pools & Travertine Terraces
  • Hierapolis Ancient Ruins
  • Cleopatra’s Pool
  • Pamukkale Antique Theatre
  • Ataturk Museum

Recommended Food

Try local specialties like gözleme, Turkish tea, and regional desserts.

Travel Tips

  • Wear non-slip shoes as terraces can be slippery.
  • Visit early morning or late afternoon for fewer crowds.
  • Combine your visit with nearby Hierapolis ruins exploration.
