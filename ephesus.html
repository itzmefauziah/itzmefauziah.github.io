Ephesus

Ephesus is one of the best-preserved ancient cities in Turkey, known for its monumental ruins and historical significance.

Main Attractions

  • Library of Celsus
  • Great Theatre
  • Temple of Artemis
  • Terrace Houses
  • Temple of Hadrian

Recommended Food

Sample local delights such as kumru sandwiches, seafood, and Turkish desserts.

Travel Tips

  • Bring sun protection as the site is mostly open-air.
  • Hire a guide to fully understand historical context.
  • Visit early to avoid large tour groups.
