Antalya

Antalya, known as the "Turkish Riviera," offers stunning beaches, ancient ruins, and a charming old town called Kaleiçi.

Main Attractions

  • Konyaaltı Beach & Lara Beach
  • Old Town (Kaleiçi)
  • Hadrian’s Gate
  • Düden Waterfalls
  • Antalya Museum

Recommended Food

Try local dishes like piyaz, grilled fish, and baklava.

Travel Tips

  • Carry swimwear for beach visits.
  • Explore old town on foot for hidden gems.
  • Visit waterfalls in the morning for best photos.
